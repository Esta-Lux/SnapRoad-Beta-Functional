  • Precise Location (GPS): Collected in real-time only during active navigation sessions. This includes your route path, speed, and heading. This is essential for turn-by-turn directions, snap-to-road functionality, and ETA calculations.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier, app version, and IP address.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, time spent in the app, screens visited, app crashes, and performance metrics. This data is anonymized where possible.
  • Navigation Data: Routes searched, destinations entered, navigation mode preferences (Calm, Adaptive, Sport), and driving analytics.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

3. Location Data — Detailed Explanation

Location data is the core of SnapRoad. Without it, navigation cannot function. Here is exactly how we handle it:

  • When collected: Only when you start a navigation session and grant location permission. We do not track your location when the app is closed or when you are not navigating.
  • Background location: If you grant “Always Allow” permission, we collect location in the background only during active navigation so turn instructions continue when the app is not in the foreground. You can change this to “While Using” at any time in your device settings.
  • How long we keep it: Location history from your trips is retained for 90 days to support features like trip summaries and driving analytics. After 90 days, location coordinates are deleted. Aggregated, anonymized data (e.g., average speed on a road segment) may be retained longer.
  • Who sees it: Your precise location is never shared with other users. If you use Family plan features like location sharing, that is a separate permission you must explicitly enable.

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can manage your preferences at any time by clicking “Cookie Settings” in the website footer.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of analytics collection and marketing communications.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously granted consent (e.g., location tracking).

To exercise any of these rights, email teams@snaproad.co with the subject line “Data Rights Request.” We will respond within 30 days.

7.1 California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out: While we do not sell personal information, you have the right to opt out of any sharing that may constitute a “sale” under CCPA.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: You may request that we limit the use of your precise geolocation data.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, email teams@snaproad.co with “CCPA Request” in the subject line.

7.2 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, your data controller is Esta Lux LLC. We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the navigation services you requested.
  • Legitimate Interest: Analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
  • Consent: Optional features like incident report photos and marketing emails.
  • Legal Obligation: Financial record keeping and regulatory compliance.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have violated your rights under GDPR.

8. Children’s Privacy

SnapRoad is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at teams@snaproad.co and we will delete such information immediately.

Our Family plan allows account holders to add teen drivers for monitoring purposes. This feature is intended for use by parents or legal guardians only.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
  • Location data is stored in encrypted databases with access limited to authorized personnel.
  • We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Payment information is processed by Stripe and Apple — we never store your full credit card number on our servers.
  • In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by law.

10. Do Not Sell My Information

SnapRoad does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data for monetary consideration. The only sharing described in this policy is with service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., Mapbox for maps, Stripe for payments).
